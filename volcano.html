A volcano is a majestic and awe-inspiring natural wonder that has captivated human imagination for centuries. It is a landform that occurs when magma from the Earth's interior rises to the surface, resulting in eruptions of molten rock, ash, and gas. Volcanoes can be towering mountains or subtle landforms, but they all share a common characteristic - the ability to shape the Earth's surface through their eruptions. As volcanoes erupt, they spew forth lava, ash, and gas, creating new landforms and altering the surrounding landscape.
